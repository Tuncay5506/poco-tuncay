Turkish Kuru Fasulye Recipe

A traditional Turkish dish of stewed white beans.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dried white beans (navy beans or cannellini beans)
  • 200g beef or lamb (optional)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper paste (optional)
  • 2 tomatoes (grated)
  • 4 cups water or broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Soak the beans in water overnight. Drain and rinse them.
  2. If using meat, sauté it in olive oil until browned, then set aside.
  3. In the same pot, sauté the chopped onion until soft.
  4. Add the tomato paste and red pepper paste, stir for 2 minutes.
  5. Add the grated tomatoes and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Return the meat (if using) to the pot, then add the soaked beans.
  7. Add water or broth, and season with salt and pepper.
  8. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to low. Simmer for about 1-1.5 hours, or until the beans are tender.
  9. Serve hot with rice and pickles.

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount (per serving)
Calories 350 kcal
Protein 15g
Carbohydrates 50g
Fat 10g
